What´s defi?

Decentralized Finance (DeFi) is the movement
that leverages decentralized networks to transform
old financial products into trustless and transparent
protocols that run without intermediaries.

Why Defi for Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is the best known and most used cryptocurrency.
In addition, it is the safest network, the one with the
highest liquidity and the one with the highest value (66% dominance).

That is why DeFi on Bitcoin will lay the foundations of the financial system of the future, in a more fair, inclusive and without intermediaries.
